About Aldersgate Youth

Our Mission

To empower young people to know Christ, grow in faith, and show God's love to the world through authentic relationships and transformative experiences.

Founded in 1995, Aldersgate Youth has been a beacon of hope and spiritual growth for generations of young believers in our community.

Our Core Values

Love

We demonstrate Christ's love through acceptance, compassion, and service to others.

Truth

We are committed to Biblical truth as the foundation for all we believe and do.

Community

We grow best together, supporting and encouraging one another in faith.
